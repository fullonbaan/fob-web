Who We Are

FullOnBaan is a specialized Infor LN ERP consulting and training company founded in 2014. We serve manufacturers, distributors, and service companies worldwide who rely on Infor LN to run their operations.

Our team of certified Infor LN consultants brings deep functional and technical expertise across the full LN suite — from Finance and Manufacturing to ION integrations and cloud deployments.

We are proud to be a trusted partner for companies ranging from mid-market manufacturers to Fortune 500 enterprises across North America, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East.

Our Mission

To make world-class Infor LN expertise accessible to every organization — regardless of size or geography — through expert consulting, implementation, and training.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted Infor LN partner globally, known for the quality of our work, integrity of our team, and impact on our clients' operations.

Our Business Model

📈 Consulting-Led

Every engagement is led by senior Infor LN consultants with 5–15+ years of hands-on experience in real implementations.

🌐 Globally Delivered

We operate across time zones, delivering remote and on-site services to clients in 20+ countries without compromising quality.

🎓 Training-Integrated

We don't just implement — we transfer knowledge. Every project includes training to build your team's internal Infor LN capability.

🎯 Outcome-Focused

We measure success by your results — go-live quality, user adoption, and long-term system performance — not just project hours.
