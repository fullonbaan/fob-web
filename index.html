INFOR Training & Certification

Who should go for this Infor LN Training?

The following professionals can go for this training:

  • Fresh Engineering Graduates
  • Technical Consultants from other ERP like SAP, Oracle
  • Functional Consultants from Finance, SCM, Manufacturing domains
  • Business Analysts, Project Managers
  • Anyone looking to switch career to ERP domain

Program Highlights & Learning Support

Dedicated Personal Trainer

Each participant is assigned a dedicated personal trainer who customizes training sessions based on individual learning objectives, availability, and pace—ensuring maximum learning effectiveness.

Curated Training eBooks

Exclusive, expertly curated Infor LN Development eBooks are provided to all participants as part of the program curriculum.

Comprehensive Training Documentation

Detailed and structured training notes are shared to reinforce key concepts and support effective revision throughout and beyond the program.

Hands-on Development Exercises

Practical, real-world development exercises are incorporated to strengthen technical proficiency and application-oriented skills.

Recorded Training Sessions

All training sessions are recorded and securely shared with participants for future reference and continued learning.

Training Completion Certificate

Participants who successfully complete the program will receive an official Training Completion Certificate from FULLONBAAN.

Dedicated WhatsApp Support Group

Participants are added to a dedicated WhatsApp support group, moderated by senior consultants, to assist with queries, project-related challenges, and ongoing guidance.
