Frequently Asked Questions

Who should take Infor LN training?

expand_more
Infor LN training is suitable for fresh engineering graduates, consultants from SAP/Oracle looking to switch to Infor LN, functional consultants in Finance, SCM or Manufacturing, business analysts, project managers, and anyone wanting to enter the ERP domain.

What does the Infor LN training program include?

expand_more
The program includes a dedicated personal trainer, curated Infor LN eBooks, comprehensive training documentation, hands-on development exercises, recorded sessions, a completion certificate, and access to a dedicated WhatsApp support group.

Is Infor LN training available online?

expand_more
Yes. FullOnBaan provides online Infor LN training with flexible scheduling. Sessions are recorded and shared with participants for future reference.

Do I get a certificate after completing Infor LN training?

expand_more
Yes. Participants who successfully complete the program receive an official Training Completion Certificate from FullOnBaan.
